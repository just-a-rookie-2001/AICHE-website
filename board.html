Dr. Snigdha Khuntia

In science and engineering, a good advisor seeks to help a student optimize an educational experience to assist their disciplinary culture. For us this figure is Dr. Snigdha Khuntia, PhD from IIT Guwahati. Her areas of interest are Advanced Oxidation Processes, Waste Water Treatment, Biosorption, Adsorption and Heavy Metal removal from waste water.
