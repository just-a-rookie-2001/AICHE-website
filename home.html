About Us

AIChE Ahmedabad University was inaugurated in August 2018 by Mr Jaimin Vasa and Dr Unnat Pandit. After that AIChE Ahmedabad University has come up a long way and has been planning events throughout and is contributing to the university community for the last 2 years. We have also organised industrial visits to many factories. In addition to serving the Chemical Engineering community, chapter has been actively collaborating with university’s other entities and clubs. Chapter has also been helping student chapters of other universities.
